ABOUT GAME


Here we have came with an awesome addicting game Running Bar. This is a single player game in which you have to move your bar with your keyboard keys. From start you have to reach at end without getting caught into LAVA. Simulataneously you have to collect golden points to score. After collecting last point your level will be completed.

Contents

  • Black vertical bar denotes current player location
  • White bar denotes the wall.
  • Red liquid denotes lava.
  • Yellow squares denote golden points.

HOW TO PLAY


Controls

  • Jump/Climb up the wall.
  • Move forward.
  • Move backward.
